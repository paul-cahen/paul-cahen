I'm a PhD student in __Mathematics__ in the field of Probability. I work under the supervision of [Christophe Garban](https://math.univ-lyon1.fr/~garban/) ([ICJ](https://www.math.univ-lyon1.fr/), Université Claude Bernard Lyon 1) and [Avelio Sepúlveda](https://www.dim.uchile.cl/~lsepulveda/) ([Universidad de Chile](https://www.dim.uchile.cl/)). You can find my CV [here](https://paul-cahen.github.io/files/CV_paul-cahen.pdf).

## Field of Research

My interests lie at the intersection of probability and statistical physics. Specifically, my research focuses on aspects of the Ising model, and related models such as FK percolation.
